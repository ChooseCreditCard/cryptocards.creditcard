What are crypto credit cards?

Crypto-linked credit cards combine traditional payment rails (Visa/Mastercard) with digital asset features such as:

  • • Rewards earned in Bitcoin or stablecoins
  • • Crypto-backed credit lines (overcollateralized)
  • • Instant settlement through stablecoin rails
  • • Web3 identity, wallets & multi-chain support

Types of crypto cards

BTC Cashback

Earn Bitcoin instead of points or miles.

Stablecoin Rewards

USDC/USDT cashback with low volatility.

Crypto Collateral

Borrow against crypto while you spend.

On-Chain Settlement

Payments cleared through L2 or stablecoin rails.

Web3 Wallet Cards

Card tied to your wallet or identity layer.
